Michigan State University Museum

CURRENT - Information Architecture Research, November-December 2016

Working with the MSU Museum, I am currently working on an information architecture research project. I am compiling a group of card sorts completed by undergraduate students regarding the content of the MSU Museum's website. While the card sorts were not originally completed under the same protocol, the data will hopefully provide insights into the effectiveness of the current sitemap. My findings will be compiled into a report and presentation in mid-December.
