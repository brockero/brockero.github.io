About Me

I'm a UX Cheerleader, UI Designer, and Front-End Developer.

I am a developer and designer by day (and some nights) and a gamer, techie, and geek by night (and most days). You'll usually find me behind my Mac, staring at my iPhone, or living a fantasy life somewhere.

I am currently a student at Michigan State University studying Experience Architecture (XA) in the College of Arts and Letters. XA is a "cutting-edge, interdisciplinary major" that exposes students to "aspects of maneuvering and creating web sites or apps such as designing, coding, writing and digital rhetoric". Basically, we learn how to create beautiful experiences for everyone. I am also studying LGBTQ and Sexuality Studies and German.

I want to work on projects that I can be proud of while continuing to advance my skills as a designer and a developer. Alongside my development and design work, I have some research experience. Go take a look at some of my projects and let's get in touch.
